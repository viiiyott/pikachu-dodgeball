# Pikachu Dodgeball

_&check;_ _English_ | [_Korean(한국어)_](README.md)

Pikachu Dogdeball is a game based on Pikachu Volleyball. We made this by editing @gorisanson 's [Web Version of Pikachu Volleyball](https://gorisanson.github.io/pikachu-volleyball/ko).

You can play this game by at https://viiiyott.github.io/pikachu-dodgeball/

## Controls

### 1P

- `D` `G` Left Right Movement
- `R` Jump
- `F` Fall
- `Z` Catch/Throw/Slide

### 2P

- `←` `→` Left Right Movement
- `↑` Jump
- `↓` Fall
- `Enter` Catch/Throw/Slide

## Rules

### Catching

- On contact with the ball, you can catch it by pressing the catch key.
- A ball thrown by the opponent is harder to catch than a ball thrown by me or a ground-ball.
- If you hold the ball for more than 3 seconds, the ball will be dropped towards the opponent by the 3 second rule.

### Throwing

- You can throw the ball by pressing the throw key while holding the ball.
- Your opponent gets hit by the ball, you get 1 point. First the 15 points is the winner.
- After the ball hits the ground, it becomes a ground-ball.
- Using the arrow keys, you can adjust what direction the ball is thrown.

### Dodging

- You can avoid the ball by jumping, falling, or sliding.
- While falling, you can adjust the falling direcion by using the arrow keys.

### Deuce

- If both players' scores are one point below the winning-score, the player who gets a two-point advantage wins.
- Deuce rules are enabled by default and can be turned off from the settings.
